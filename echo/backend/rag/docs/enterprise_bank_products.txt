SAVINGS ACCOUNTS
================

- Features: Online banking, debit card, checkbook, overdraft facility (up to Rs. 1 lakh)
- Minimum Balance: Rs. 1,000 (Rs. 2,500 in metros)

- Charges: Annual account maintenance Rs. 0 (free), minimum balance penalty Rs. 50/quarter if below
- Debit Card: Free lifetime, EMV chip + contactless enabled
- Standing Instructions: Unlimited, free monthly/custom recurring transfers
- Overdraft Facility: Subject to credit score, interest 2% above savings rate
- Who Should Open: Salary account holders, regular savers, beginners

FIXED DEPOSITS
==============

Standard Fixed Deposit:
- Deposit Amount: Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1 crore per FD
- Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
- Interest Rates (Current): 7.25% (7 days) to 9% (3+ years), senior citizens +0.5%
- Compounding: Quarterly compounding (interest on interest)
- Payout Options: At maturity, monthly interest, or monthly + principal at end
- Premature Withdrawal: Allowed after 7 days, with 1% (6-month tenor) to 0.5% (>6-month tenor) penalty
- Loan Against FD: Available at FD rate + 2%, up to 90% of FD value
- Auto-Renewal: Can be set up to auto-renew at maturity at prevailing rates
- Tax: TDS at 10% on interest if exceeds Rs. 10,000 annually
- Insurance: Deposits up to Rs. 1 lakh covered under DICGC deposit insurance
- Digital FD: Available from Rs. 100 online through mobile app

Senior Citizen Fixed Deposit:
- Deposit Amount: Rs. 10,000 minimum
- Tenure: 5 years (special tenure for this category)
- Interest Rate: 9.5% p.a. (0.5% premium over standard FD)
- Additional Benefit: 0.25% additional bonus if auto-renewed consecutively
- Priority Renewal: Special window at maturity with personal call from relationship manager
- Loan Facility: Higher LTV - up to 95% against FD value
- Insurance: Full deposit covered under DICGC insurance
- Features: Monthly interest payout option (especially for pensioners)
- Minimum Maturity: 1 year, no premature withdrawal penalty in first 12 months

Flexi Deposits (with variable maturity):
- Deposit Amount: Rs. 25,000 to Rs. 1 crore
- Tenure: 1 year (convertible every year for up to 5 years)
- Interest Rate: 8% for year 1, increases 0.5% per year (year 2: 8.5%, year 3: 9%, etc.)
- Benefit: Lock in higher rates as tenure increases
- Partial Withdrawal: Can withdraw up to 50% of principal after 1 year without penalty
- Ladder Strategy: Common for customers wanting to stagger maturity dates

Foreign Currency Deposits:
- Currencies Accepted: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, SGD, AED, and 5 others
- Deposit Amount: Equivalent to Rs. 50,000 minimum
- Interest Rate: Currency-specific rates (USD: 4.5%, EUR: 3%, etc.)
- Tenure: 1 year to 5 years
- Benefit: Earn interest in foreign currency, hedge against INR depreciation
- Who Should Open: NRI, business owners with foreign exposure

RECURRING DEPOSITS
==================

Standard Recurring Deposit (RD):
- Monthly Deposit: Rs. 500 to Rs. 10 lakhs per month
- Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
- Interest Rate: Same as FD for equivalent maturity (quarterly compounding)
- Calculation: Interest calculated on accumulated balance (not on individual deposits)
- Maturity: Lump sum of total deposits + accumulated interest
- Default Handling: Auto-retry if first debit fails, no penalty first 3 months
- Loan Against RD: Available at RD rate + 2%
- Senior Citizen RD: Additional 0.5% interest, auto-withdrawal option
- Features: Flexible tenure adjustment, can pause for up to 6 months
- Tax: TDS on interest as per Income Tax Act

Flexi RD (Graduated RD):
- Monthly Deposit: Can increase by predefined amount each month/year
- Example: Start with Rs. 1,000, increase by 10% yearly (Rs. 1,100, Rs. 1,210, etc.)
- Benefit: Savings grow over time as income increases, better returns
- Tenure: 12 months minimum

DEPOSIT SCHEMES
===============

Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP):
- Deposit Amount: Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 1.5 lakhs per person per financial year
- Tenure: 124 months (10 years, 4 months)
- Interest Rate: 7.7% p.a. compounded annually
- Return: Initial investment doubles in tenure
- Tax: Exempt under Section 80C
- Who Can Open: Indian citizens, minors (through guardians), joint accounts

Sukanya Samriddhi Scheme (SSA):
- Eligibility: Girl child (18 months to 10 years old)
- Minimum Deposit: Rs. 250 per year (minimum), Rs. 1.5 lakhs (maximum)
- Interest Rate: 8.2% p.a. compounded annually
- Tenure: 21 years from opening or 18 years from maturity (girl's age 18+)
- Tax Benefit: Section 80C deduction
- Guardianship: Opened by parent/guardian on behalf of girl child
